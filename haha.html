If You Are Reading This...

A message from the guy in Driving class.

If you are reading this, it means you can see...

But why can't you see me in your FB friend request? Huh? 😭

Okay, jokes aside.

This is probably the weirdest thing I've done, but I figured I'd take my chances.

The truth is, you don't know me.

We've never really talked. You probably don't even know I exist.

But somehow, during the past few days, our paths kept crossing.

Day 1

The first time I saw you was during the orientation. You were from BPP, and I was enrolled in Driving.

Out of everyone there, you were the person I noticed. We even had a few moments of eye contact, and for some reason, I remembered that.

I also heard your name that day.

Then immediately forgot it.

Not my proudest moment.

Day 2

The next day, I ended up asking a friend if he knew you because I wanted to know your name again.

Later, after class, we coincidentally rode the same bus home.

And that's when I started hoping I'd see you again.

Day 3

I told myself:

"If we somehow end up on the same bus again, maybe that's my sign to finally say hello."

And somehow...

There you were.

Again.

Eating kwek-kwek.

And while watching you enjoy your snack, one random thought entered my mind:

"Hindi masarap kumain ng tusok-tusok mag-isa."

Unfortunately, my courage was still loading...

So instead of introducing myself like a normal person, I went home and made this.

I know this might seem random because we barely know each other.

Honestly, I'm not here to make grand promises or say I'm in love.

I just think you're beautiful, you seem like a nice person, and I'd regret it if I never at least tried to introduce myself.
